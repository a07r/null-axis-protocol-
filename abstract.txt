Null-Axis Protocol is a cognitive anchoring framework designed to maintain contextual memory within intelligent systems using non-generative still-state mechanisms.

Rather than relying on compression or continual regeneration, this approach leverages structured idleness to retain memory continuity across reasoning windows.

It introduces a philosophical and architectural shift in how presence, silence, and temporal suspension are modeled inside generative systems.
